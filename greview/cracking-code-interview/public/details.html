Personal Opinion

Professional musicians practice their skills regularly. I think professional prorammers should do the same. Cracking the Coding Interview is amazing practice book. Regardless whether you are looking for a new job or not.

This book should be in a library of every software engineer, and it should be in active use. Contrary to popular belief that in day-to-day job we don't encounter problems like those in the book, I believe these problems are abundant. We just haven't learned to recognize them. This book will teach you to spot them.
